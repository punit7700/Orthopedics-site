Dr. Apoorv Dua is a highly skilled orthopedic surgeon renowned for his expertise in hip, knee, and shoulder replacement and reconstruction. With a patient-centric approach and years of experience, he provides comprehensive care in the following areas:

+ Joint Replacement & Reconstruction

Comprehensive care for hip, knee, and shoulder joint replacement surgeries using advanced techniques to restore mobility, reduce pain, and improve quality of life.

+ Arthroscopic & Sports Injury Treatments

Minimally invasive arthroscopic procedures for ligament injuries, meniscus tears, and sports-related trauma, ensuring faster recovery and optimal performance.

+ Advanced Regenerative & Non-Surgical Treatments

State-of-the-art regenerative therapies such as PRP and stem-cell-based treatments aimed at healing tissues naturally and avoiding surgery whenever possible.

+ Traumatology & Complex Fracture Management

Expert management of complex fractures and trauma cases using evidence-based surgical and non-surgical approaches for optimal bone healing and recovery.
